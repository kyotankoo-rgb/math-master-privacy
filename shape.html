Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Introduction

Shape Master ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our application.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Player names (for leaderboard)
  • High scores
  • Game settings (language preference)
  • Goal settings

This data is stored using your browser's localStorage and never leaves your device.

Data Sharing

We do not directly share, sell, or transmit your personal data to third parties. However, the third-party services described below may collect certain information as part of their operation.

Third-Party Services

This app uses the following third-party services:

Advertising (Google AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use the following information:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • IP address (for approximate location)
  • Ad interaction data

For more information, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

In-App Purchases (Google Play Billing)

This app offers an optional in-app purchase to remove advertisements. All purchases are processed through Google Play's billing system. We do not collect or store any payment information. For details, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

This app is designed to be family-friendly. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Advertisements displayed through AdMob comply with Google's policies for family-friendly content.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our GitHub repository.
